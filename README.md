# mitoribosomalProteins
Repository of scripts used for a study: A pan-eukaryotic analysis of mitochondrial ribosomes uncovers major evolutionary transitions


During the preparation of this work, the authors used OpenAI ChatGPT (versions 5 to 5.5, August 2025–May 2026) to assist in generating and reviewing scripts for data analysis. All AI-generated content was reviewed, edited, and verified by the authors, who take full responsibility for the accuracy and integrity of the published article.
